Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

1 pound pasta of your choice
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes with basil
1/4 cup grated fresh mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated fresh parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic, minced
Fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
In a large skillet, heat the diced tomatoes with basil over medium heat.
Add the fresh mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese is melted.
Add the cooked pasta to the tomato sauce and toss to coat evenly.
Add the minced garlic and chopped basil leaves (if using) and toss again.
Serve hot and enjoy!
